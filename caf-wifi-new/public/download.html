Windows Agent DOWNLOAD READY
Run on your PC — phone connects to it for real WiFi data
Step 1: Download both files below to the same folder
Step 2: Double-click caf-wifi-agent.bat
Step 3: Agent shows your PC IP (e.g. 172.16.46.148)
Step 4: In app on phone → Connect Agent → enter:
    http://172.16.46.148:7788/api/scan
Step 5: Real WiFi networks appear on your phone ✅
⬇ caf-wifi-agent.bat ⬇ caf-wifi-agent.js
